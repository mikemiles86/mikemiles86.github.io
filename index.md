I’m Mike Miles, a technical leader, speaker, and longtime open-source practitioner focused on modern web development, technical leadership, and sustainable delivery.

I serve as the Director of Web Development at [MIT Sloan](https://mitsloan.mit.edu), where I lead the development, maintenance, and growth of the school’s public digital properties, along with the team that supports them.

[View my talks](https://talks.mike-miles.com/) · [Learn more about MIT Sloan](https://mitsloan.mit.edu)


## What I focus on

- modern web development and open-source delivery
- technical leadership and team growth
- testing, quality, and long-term maintainability
- practical, responsible use of AI in development work


## Speaking

I regularly speak on Drupal, web development, technical leadership, testing, delivery practices, and practical AI adoption.

You can explore past talks and upcoming presentations on my [Talks](https://talks.mike-miles.com/) page.
